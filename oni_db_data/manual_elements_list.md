## Klingon Vocabulary Strategy:

### Gases (Life Support)
- Oxygen → tlhol (life-giving air)
- Carbon Dioxide → tlholQe' (false air)
- Hydrogen → bIQ'a' (great water)
- Steam → bIQ tuj (hot water)

### Liquids (Water-based)
- Water → bIQ
- Polluted Water → bIQ qagh (contaminated water)
- Salt Water → bIQ tach (salty water)
- Crude Oil → qettlh (petroleum)

### Solids (Building Materials)
- Iron → baS (metal)
- Steel → DIch baS (type-2 metal)
- Sand → tlhargh (sand)
- Glass → qoD (transparent material)

### Food
- Meallice → 'opwI' (small grain)
- Mush Bar → tIr ghoD (mushy grain bar)
- Berry Sludge → naH rIQ (crushed fruit)
